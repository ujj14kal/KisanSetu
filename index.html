How It Works

Simple for everyone

Two roles, one platform. Farmers get found, landlords hire directly.

For Farmers

Get hired directly

Showcase your skills and availability, get discovered by verified employers, and build a credible work history — no agent fees, ever.

01
Create your profile

Sign up with your mobile number. Add your skills, location, and availability in minutes.

02
Verify with Aadhaar

OTP-based identity verification builds trust with employers and confirms your profile is genuine.

03
Accept or decline requests

Landlords send you hiring requests. Review each one from your dashboard and decide on your terms.

Why Kisan Setu

Built around trust

Every design decision on this platform was made to protect farmers and landlords from exploitation.

Aadhaar-verified profiles

Every user verifies their identity via Aadhaar OTP before their profile becomes visible. No fake accounts, no anonymous actors.

No agents, no commissions

Farmers and landlords communicate directly on the platform. There are no brokers taking a cut from either side.

Transparent job history

Every accepted request is recorded. Farmers build credibility over time. Landlords can see a farmer's track record before hiring.

In-app chat after hiring

Once a request is accepted, both parties can communicate through encrypted in-platform chat — no need to share personal numbers.
