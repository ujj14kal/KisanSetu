Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective for all Kisan Setu users

Kisan Setu ("we", "our", "the platform") is operated in India and is committed to protecting the personal information of all users — farmers and landlords — in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable UIDAI guidelines.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity data: Full name, age, mobile number, email address.
  • Aadhaar data: Scanned images of the front and back of your Aadhaar card, collected solely for identity verification purposes. We do not store your Aadhaar number in our database.
  • Profile data: Location, farm type, crops grown, skills, and availability.
  • Usage data: Hiring requests sent/received, job history, and in-app chat messages.
  • Payment data: Razorpay subscription ID and payment confirmation. We do not store card or UPI details — these are handled entirely by Razorpay.
  • Device data: Browser type, IP address, and session token for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account on Kisan Setu.
  • To verify your identity via Aadhaar documents before making your profile visible to others.
  • To connect farmers with landlords through hiring requests and in-app chat.
  • To process landlord subscription payments via Razorpay.
  • To send transactional notifications (account activity, hiring request updates).
  • To improve platform safety and detect fraudulent activity.

3. Aadhaar Data — Special Obligations

We collect Aadhaar card images only with your explicit consent (provided via the consent checkbox during registration) and strictly for the purpose of identity verification. In accordance with UIDAI guidelines:

  • Aadhaar images are stored in a private, access-controlled Supabase storage bucket and are not publicly accessible.
  • Only authorised Kisan Setu staff review these documents for verification.
  • We do not share Aadhaar data with any third party.
  • You may request deletion of your Aadhaar documents at any time by contacting us or using the "Delete Account" option in your dashboard.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in the following limited cases:

  • Between users: Your profile (name, location, skills, Aadhaar-verified badge) is visible to landlords who search the platform. Your contact details (mobile, email) are not exposed.
  • Service providers: Supabase (database and storage), Firebase (phone OTP), and Razorpay (payments). Each operates under its own privacy policy and data processing agreements.
  • Legal obligations: If required by law or court order.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile data is removed within 30 days. Aadhaar images are deleted within 7 days of an account deletion request. Anonymised usage statistics may be retained for longer for platform analytics.

6. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act, 2023

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction: Update incorrect information via your dashboard or by contacting us.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your account and personal data, including Aadhaar images.
  • Right to grievance redressal: Raise a complaint with our Grievance Officer (details below).
  • Right to nominate: Nominate a person to exercise your data rights in case of death or incapacity.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store session tokens and temporary login hints (e.g., your selected role). No third-party advertising cookies are used on this platform.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Supabase enforces Row Level Security (RLS) on all database tables so users can only access their own data. Aadhaar documents are stored in a private bucket inaccessible via public URLs. Payments are verified server-side via cryptographic signature check.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in law or our practices. We will notify users of material changes via the email address on their account. Continued use of the platform after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
