How Kisan Setu Works

A transparent, secure process for verified farm work opportunities

For Farmers (Job Seekers)

Farmers and agricultural workers can create a verified profile on Kisan Setu and get hired by trusted employers without intermediaries.

1. Create Your Account

Sign up using your mobile number. A One-Time Password (OTP) is sent to ensure secure access. This ensures every user on Kisan Setu is authentic.

2. Complete Verification

Upload your Aadhaar details for identity verification. Verification is done securely using OTP-based confirmation to maintain privacy and trust.

3. Build Your Profile

Add details such as work experience, preferred type of farm work, availability, and location. A complete profile improves your chances of getting hired.

4. Get Discovered by Employers

Verified employers browse worker profiles based on their requirements. Your profile is shown only to verified users to ensure safety.

5. Track Hiring Requests

When an employer is interested, you receive a notification. You can accept or decline requests directly from your dashboard.

6. Work with Confidence

Once hired, work details remain visible in your hiring history. This builds credibility and helps with future opportunities.

For Employers (Hiring Workers)

Employers can find reliable, verified agricultural workers quickly and transparently.

1. Register & Verify

Create an account using your mobile number and complete identity verification to access verified worker profiles.

2. Search for Workers

Browse worker profiles using filters like location, skill type, availability, and experience.

3. View Verified Profiles

Access verified profiles with essential details while respecting privacy. Full details are available only to verified employers.

4. Send Hiring Request

Contact workers directly through the platform by sending a hiring request. No agents or middlemen involved.

5. Manage Hiring

Track accepted and rejected requests, manage ongoing work, and maintain a hiring history for future reference.
