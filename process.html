Platform Guide

How Kisan Setu Works

Two roles, one transparent process. Here's exactly what happens from sign-up to hire — no hidden steps, no agents.

The Process

Step by step, for both sides

Farmers get found, landlords hire directly. The entire flow is designed to remove intermediaries.

For Farmers

Get hired directly

Build a verified profile, get discovered by employers, and manage your work history — all without paying anyone a commission.

01
Sign up with your mobile number

Register using your phone. An OTP is sent to verify you're a real person before your account is created.

02
Verify your identity with Aadhaar

Complete Aadhaar OTP verification. This is what makes your profile trusted — employers can see you're a real, verified person.

03
Build your profile

Add your farming skills, preferred work types, location, and availability. Upload a photo. The more complete your profile, the more visible you are.

04
Get discovered by landlords

Verified landlords search profiles by location and skill. Your profile appears in results automatically once complete.

05
Accept or decline hiring requests

When a landlord is interested, you receive a hiring request in your dashboard. Review the details and respond on your own terms.

06
Build your work history

Every accepted request is logged. Over time this builds your credibility and helps you get hired faster in the future.

For Landlords

Hire with confidence

Search verified farmer profiles, send direct hiring requests, and keep a clear record of every engagement — no brokers, no commissions.

01
Register and verify

Create your account with your mobile number and complete Aadhaar verification. This unlocks access to verified farmer profiles.

02
Activate your subscription

A simple ₹49/month subscription gives landlords full access to search and contact farmers. No hidden charges, cancel anytime.

03
Search farmers by location and skill

Filter profiles by district, work type, and availability. Every profile you see is Aadhaar-verified — no anonymous or fake accounts.

04
Send a direct hiring request

Contact farmers directly through the platform. No agent fees, no middlemen. Just a straightforward request the farmer can accept or decline.

05
Communicate via in-app chat

Once a request is accepted, both parties can message through encrypted in-platform chat. No need to share personal phone numbers.

Our Guarantee

What's true at every step

These principles are non-negotiable, regardless of who you are or where you are on the platform.

No fake profiles

Every account on the platform is Aadhaar-verified before it becomes visible to others.

Zero agent commissions

Farmers keep 100% of what they earn. Landlords pay a flat platform fee, not a cut per hire.

Transparent history

Every hiring request — accepted or declined — is recorded so both sides have a clear, auditable record.
